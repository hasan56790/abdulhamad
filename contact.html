Yes, I work with clients from all over the world. I use modern collaboration tools like Slack, Trello, and Zoom to ensure smooth communication and project management regardless of location.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and hourly rates for ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements. After understanding your needs, I'll provide a transparent quote with no hidden costs.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, and technical support.
